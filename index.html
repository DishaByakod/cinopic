“A SHOT THAT RESONATES IN EVERY CLIENT’S HEART AND SHINES WITH GRACE IN EVERY ARTIST’S PORTFOLIO.”

Allow me to introduce myself—I am Sukany Jayasekar, a devotedvisual artist whose journey into photography began in childhood and evolved into an unwavering pursuit of cinematographic excellence. My affinity for cameras is both innate and profound, an enduring passion that has guided me into the elite world of cinema,where each frame is a carefully crafted narrative, and every shot is a work of art.

My academic journey has been meticulously curated through advanced studies in filmmaking, complemented by specialized training in the art of cinematography. These experiences have not only honed my technical mastery but also enriched my aesthetic sensibilities. However, it is the invaluable mentorship from cinematic luminaries that has truly refined my vision, shaping me into the Director of Photography (DOP) I am today. Under their tutelage, I’ve had the privilege of contributing to an array of distinguished short films and feature productions.

For me, capturing the perfect frame is an art of precision and elegance—a symphony of light, texture, and emotion. It is in the subtle shifts of perspective that the extraordinary emerges, turning the ordinary into the sublime. Each image I create serves as atestament to the power of visual storytelling, an ode to the beauty of life viewed through the lens of luxury and refinement.
